## Area of a Rectangle

The area of a rectangle can be calculated by multiplying the lengths of the two perpendicular sides together.

```math
Area = width x height
```

**For Example**

For a rectangle where *width = 3* and *length = 4*

```math
Area = width x length = 3 x 4 = 12
```
